Meet our Founder

The Founder and Executive Director of HDA Foundation, Olatayo Lola Emmanuel Esq. happens to be a child of a fallen hero. Her father was NWO Emmanuel Kayode Oloye, who served in the Nigerian Navy before his demise in active service. Olatayo was only 12 years old when her father passed and was introduced into a new world of being raised alone by her widowed mother with the aid of family, friends and the military through its aid schemes.
